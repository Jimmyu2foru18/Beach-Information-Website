Long Beach & Jones Beach

Long Island's Premier Coastal Destinations

The iconic Long Beach Boardwalk stretches 2.2 miles

Overview

Long Beach and Jones Beach are two of Long Island's most beloved coastal destinations, offering miles of pristine shoreline, recreational activities, and rich history.

Beach Access

Open year-round with lifeguards from Memorial Day to Labor Day

Parking

Ample parking available at both beaches with seasonal permits

Activities

Surfing, swimming, fishing, biking, and volleyball

Long Beach

Long Beach's pristine shoreline

Boardwalk Highlights

  • 2.2-mile iconic boardwalk
  • Restaurants and concession stands
  • Exercise stations and bike rentals
  • Regular events and festivals

Jones Beach

Jones Beach State Park's expansive shoreline

Park Amenities

  • 6.5 miles of beach
  • Jones Beach Theater
  • Nature center and walking trails
  • Mini golf and games

Beach Passes

Daily and seasonal passes available. Residents and non-residents have different rates.
